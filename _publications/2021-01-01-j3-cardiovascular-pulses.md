This paper presents a radar-based non-contact method for estimating cardiovascular pulses to screen for dengue fever. We employ continuous-wave Doppler radar combined with empirical mode decomposition (EMD) and continuous wavelet transform (CWT) for accurate short-time pulse estimation.
