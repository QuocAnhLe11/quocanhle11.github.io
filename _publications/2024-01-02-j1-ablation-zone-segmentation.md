This paper develops a semi-automatic method to segment the liver ablation zone in CT images after thermal ablation for liver cancer treatment. We utilize 3D nn-UNet to segment the ablation zone coarsely; the incorrectly segmented region is then refined by user click points using RITM — an interactive segmentation method. The proposed method achieves state-of-the-art performance on ablation segmentation accuracy; on average, only 2 minutes are required to correct the segmentation.
