I am a Lecturer and PhD Student at the [Faculty of Electronics and Telecommunications](https://fet.vnu.edu.vn/), [VNU University of Engineering and Technology](https://uet.vnu.edu.vn/) (VNU-UET), Hanoi, Vietnam. My PhD thesis focuses on **spectral analysis and federated learning for neurological disorders**, supervised by Prof. Nguyen Linh Trung (VNU-UET) and Prof. Karim Abed-Meraim (Université d'Orléans).

I received my Master's degree in Communication and Data Engineering from [Paris-Saclay University](https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/) (GPA: 14.24/20) in 2024, and my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from VNU-UET (GPA: 3.43/4.0, Top 10%) in 2020.

## Research Interests

- **Medical Image Analysis**: Image processing and deep learning for lesion classification, detection, and segmentation to assist physicians in diagnosis.
- **Biomedical Signal Processing**: Advanced signal processing methods for feature engineering from biomedical signals (EEG, radar-based vital signs) to improve disease detection and prognosis.
- **Tensor Decomposition**: Novel tensor decomposition approaches to uncover hidden features in high-dimensional biomedical data.
